Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our ID verification and compliance services.

Do you accept non-UK passports and driving licences?
Yes, we accept passports and driving licences from all countries that meet international standards. Our system supports over 200 different document types and automatically detects the document type for verification. We can verify documents from EU countries, Commonwealth nations, and most other jurisdictions.
Is the entire process fully remote?
Absolutely. The entire verification process is completed remotely through our secure mobile portal. No physical meetings, document posting, or in-person verification required. Your clients can complete everything from their mobile device or computer.
What's the typical turnaround time for verification?
Most verifications are completed within 2-4 hours during business hours. Complex cases or additional screening may take up to 24 hours. You'll receive email notifications at each stage of the process, and our dashboard provides real-time status updates.
How long do you retain verification data?
We retain verification data for a minimum of 5 years as required by UK Money Laundering Regulations 2017. All data is encrypted and stored securely in UK-based data centres. You can request data deletion after the retention period, subject to regulatory requirements.
Do you handle Companies House filings automatically?
Yes, for Company pack customers, we automatically submit director appointment and PSC register updates to Companies House on your behalf. This includes all necessary forms and documentation. You'll receive confirmation once filings are submitted and accepted.
What PEP and sanctions screening do you perform?
We screen against UK and international PEP lists, sanctions lists, and adverse media databases. This includes HM Treasury sanctions, EU sanctions, UN sanctions, and other relevant databases. Any matches are flagged for manual review and escalation if necessary.
What are your SAR (Suspicious Activity Report) procedures?
We have established procedures for identifying and reporting suspicious activity to the National Crime Agency (NCA). Our compliance team reviews all flagged cases and submits SARs where appropriate. We maintain strict confidentiality and follow no tipping-off protocols.
How do you ensure data protection and GDPR compliance?
We are ICO registered and fully GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, stored in UK-based data centres, and processed according to our comprehensive data processing policies. We have appointed a Data Protection Officer and maintain detailed records of processing activities.
What does ACSP supervision mean for your services?
As an ACSP (Anti-Money Laundering and Counter-Terrorist Financing Supervised Person), we are supervised by CGI UK & Ireland under the Money Laundering Regulations 2017. This ensures our processes meet the highest regulatory standards and provides additional assurance for our clients.
Can you integrate with our existing systems?
Yes, we offer API integration for enterprise customers and can work with your existing case management, CRM, or compliance systems. We also provide webhook notifications and can export data in various formats to suit your requirements.

Our compliance team is here to help with any specific requirements.
