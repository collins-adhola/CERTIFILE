Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our ID verification process.

Do you accept non-UK passports?
Yes, we accept passports from all countries that meet international standards. Our system supports over 200 different passport types and automatically detects the document type for verification.
Is the process fully remote?
Absolutely. The entire verification process is completed remotely through our secure mobile portal. No physical meetings or document posting required.
What's the typical turnaround time?
Most verifications are completed within 2-4 hours during business hours. Complex cases or additional screening may take up to 24 hours. You'll receive email notifications at each stage.
How long do you retain data?
We retain verification data for 7 years as required by UK regulations. All data is encrypted and stored securely. You can request data deletion after the retention period.
Do you handle Companies House filings?
Yes, we can submit director appointment and PSC register updates to Companies House on your behalf. This is included in our Company pack pricing.
