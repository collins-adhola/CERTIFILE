What's included in our service

Our fixed fee includes:

  • Verification using any standard form of ID
  • Access to our admin dashboard to track progress
  • Advice on completing verification for you and your stakeholders
  • Registering with Companies House and obtaining the Verification ID
  • Liaising with Companies House to resolve any issues

Notes and assumptions

For our fees, we assume:

  • Client Responsibility: The fee assumes that the client provides complete, accurate, and timely information necessary for successful verification
  • Re-verification Costs: Any re-verification, required by external regulations or additional requests, is considered a separate service and is not part of the headline fee
  • VAT and Additional Charges: The fee does not include VAT and may exclude any interest on late payments or extra charges for work beyond the initial scope
  • Non-refundable Fees: In cases where the verification is not achieved to the required standards, the fee remains non-refundable

All prices exclude VAT and third-party fees. Volume discounts available for larger organisations.
